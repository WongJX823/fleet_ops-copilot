# SOP-02: Delay Management

## Purpose
Consistent handling of trip delays so passengers, drivers, and connecting services get timely, accurate information.

## Thresholds
- Under 10 minutes: monitor only; no announcement required.
- 10 to 29 minutes: publish a delay notice on affected stops and update the trip status.
- 30 minutes or more: treat as a disruption; open an incident and consider a relief vehicle.

## Dispatch steps
1. Confirm the delay cause and expected duration from the driver or telematics.
2. Update the trip status with the delay in minutes and the reason.
3. Notify connecting services if the delay breaks a guaranteed connection.
4. For recurring congestion delays on the same corridor, flag the route to planning for schedule review.

## Communication rules
- Announcements must state the route, direction, expected delay, and the information timestamp.
- Never announce an estimate older than 10 minutes without re-confirming.
