# SOP-01: Vehicle Breakdown in Service

## Purpose
Standard procedure when a revenue vehicle becomes unserviceable before or during a trip.

## Immediate steps (driver)
1. Stop the vehicle in a safe location and activate hazard lights.
2. Report the breakdown to dispatch via radio or the driver app, stating vehicle ID, location, and fault symptoms.
3. Keep passengers informed; do not attempt roadside repairs beyond resetting a tripped breaker.

## Dispatch steps
1. Log the breakdown as an incident with vehicle ID, trip ID, and location.
2. Check the fleet system for the nearest **available** vehicle of a compatible type.
3. Assign a replacement vehicle and, if the rostered driver cannot continue, a standby driver.
4. If no replacement is available within 30 minutes, cancel the trip and notify affected connections.
5. Arrange recovery of the failed vehicle to the nearest workshop.

## Approval rules
- Replacement assignment within the same depot: dispatcher may approve.
- Cross-depot reassignment or trip cancellation: requires operations manager approval.
